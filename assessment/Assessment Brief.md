\# BI Developer Assessment



\## Scenario



Atlas Fuel Services Ltd has asked you to perform an initial commercial review of its business.



You have access to a SQL Server database containing customer, transaction, product, network, account manager, target and customer service case data.



Your task is to explore the data using SQL, identify commercially useful insights, and build a short Power BI report suitable for presentation to senior management.



There is no single correct answer.



We are interested in how you approach an unfamiliar dataset, the questions you choose to ask, and how clearly you communicate your findings.



Assume this is your first week working for Atlas Fuel Services. Build the report you believe would help you understand the business and support a discussion with the Commercial Director.



\---



\## Expected Duration



Approximately 2–3 hours.



\---



\## Assessment Task



Using SQL, investigate the data and identify the insights that you believe would be most valuable to the Commercial Director.



Once you have completed your SQL analysis:



1\. Use the outputs of your SQL analysis to build a concise Power BI report.

2\. Be prepared to present your findings to the interview panel.



\---



\## Suggested Areas to Explore



These are suggestions only. You do not need to cover every area.



\- Customer performance

\- Product performance

\- Network performance

\- Regional performance

\- Account Manager performance

\- Customer service activity

\- Revenue, volume and margin trends

\- Data quality observations

\- Any other insight you believe is commercially important



\---



\## Presentation



Be prepared to discuss:



\- What you investigated

\- Why you chose those areas

\- The SQL queries you wrote

\- What your Power BI report demonstrates

\- The key commercial insights you identified

\- Any data quality issues you discovered

\- What recommendations you would make to senior management

\- What additional information you would request if this were a real business project



\---



\## Deliverables



Please bring:



\- Your SQL queries

\- Your completed Power BI report (.pbix)

\- Any supporting files used during your analysis



\---



\## What We Are Assessing



We will assess your ability to:



\- Explore and understand an unfamiliar dataset

\- Write clear and effective SQL

\- Build a concise and informative Power BI report

\- Identify meaningful commercial insights

\- Recognise potential data quality issues

\- Communicate your findings clearly and confidently



There is no expectation that every candidate will investigate the same areas or produce the same report. We are interested in your approach, reasoning and ability to translate data into meaningful business insight.
