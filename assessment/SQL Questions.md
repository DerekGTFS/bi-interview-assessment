# SQL Interview Questions

You are working for Atlas Fuel Services Ltd, a fictional fuel-card provider.

The Commercial Director is worried that the business is growing volume but losing margin quality.

Use SQL to investigate and prepare a short verbal summary of your findings.

## Tables

- interview.Customers
- interview.Transactions
- interview.Products
- interview.Networks
- interview.AccountManagers
- interview.Targets
- interview.Cases
- interview.vwCommercialSales

## Questions

1. What are the total Quantity, Revenue, NetRevenue and Margin PPL by month?

2. Which account managers have the highest volume but weakest Margin PPL?

3. Which customers are high-volume but low-margin?

4. Which networks are driving the most volume, and are any networks commercially weaker than others?

5. Compare monthly actuals against target by account manager.

6. Identify customers who were active historically but appear dormant in the last 90 days.

7. Which industries are growing or declining over time?

8. Are there any data quality issues you would raise before building a dashboard?

9. Create one query that you would use as a clean reporting dataset for Power BI.

10. Summarise three commercial recommendations you would give to the Sales Director.
