# Candidate Brief

You have joined Atlas Fuel Services Ltd as a BI Developer.

Atlas sells fuel cards, EV charging and fleet-related products to UK businesses. The business tracks:

- fuel volume
- revenue
- net revenue / margin
- margin pence per litre
- customer activity
- account manager performance
- network performance
- monthly targets

The Commercial Director has asked:

> "Are we growing profitably, or are we just chasing volume?"

You have 60-90 minutes to explore the database, write SQL, and prepare a short summary of what you found.

This is not a memory test. We are interested in how you think, how you structure SQL, how you check data quality, and whether you can turn data into commercial insight.
